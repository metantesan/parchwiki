** opens terminal **
``super + return``

** opens file manager **
``super + shift + return ``

** opens dmenu **
``super + p``

** opens logout window **
``super + shift + q``

** opens dm_hub for screenshot or etc **
``super + shift + x``

** kill active window **
``super + shift + c``

** cycle between the workspaces **
``super + 1-9``

** move active window to another workspace **
`` super + shift + 1-9 ``
